Streamlined Workflow

1

Intake & Hydration

The inventory team receives a shipment and hydrates the flower for 2-3 days.

2

Breakdown

After hydration, the team breaks down the entire 908g batch into the specified Jars (for bud tenders) and the 70 pre-packaged Bags (for bulk storage).

3

Stock & Transfer

All 70 pre-packaged bags will be placed in the bulk section at Kirkwood. The 50g jars for Booths and Columbus are prepared for transfer.

4

Bud Tender Role

Bud tenders at each location use their designated jars (300g at Kirkwood, 50g at others) to create fresh 1g bags for front display and back stock. This separates bulk packaging from on-demand customer service.
