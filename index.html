Streamlined Workflow

1

Intake & Hydration

The inventory team receives a shipment and hydrates the flower for 2-3 days.

2

Breakdown

After hydration, the team breaks down the entire batch (based on its size) into the specified Jars (for bud tenders) and the pre-packaged Bags (for bulk storage).

3

Stock & Transfer

All pre-packaged bags will be placed in the bulk section at Kirkwood. The jars for Booths and Columbus are prepared for transfer.

4

Bud Tender Role

Bud tenders at each location use their designated jars (size depends on the batch) to create fresh 1g bags for front display and back stock. This separates bulk packaging from on-demand customer service.
